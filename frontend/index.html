Frequently asked questions

Is there a free trial?
Yes. All Growth plan features are available for 14 days with no credit card required. You can import your real orders and inventory to see exactly how Nexus works for your business.
Can I switch plans later?
Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. When you upgrade, you get access to the new features immediately and are billed pro-rata.
What happens if I exceed my order limit?
We'll notify you as you approach your limit. We never block order processing mid-cycle. Excess orders are billed at a small per-order fee until you upgrade.
Do you charge per channel integration?
No. Growth and Enterprise plans include unlimited channel integrations at no extra cost. Your platform cost should never increase just because you add a new sales channel.
How does onboarding work?
All Growth and Enterprise customers get a dedicated onboarding specialist. We migrate your existing data, set up channel integrations, and train your team. Most customers go live within 2-3 business days.
Is my data secure?
Yes. Nexus is hosted on AWS India (Mumbai region), is SOC 2 Type II certified, and uses AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. Enterprise plans support SSO and audit logs.
Our story

We're building the operating system
for Indian commerce.

Founded in 2021 in Bengaluru, Nexus was born from a frustration — that India's fastest-growing brands were stitching together 12 different tools just to run their operations.

Our mission
"To make world-class commerce operations accessible to every Indian brand, not just the ones with 50-person ops teams."

We started by talking to hundreds of ops managers, warehouse supervisors, and founders. We saw the same pain everywhere — disconnected tools, missed SLAs, oversells, and hours wasted on manual reconciliation.

Today, we process over 12 million orders a day for 500+ brands, and we're just getting started.
